The REPL

When you invoke scala with no arguments, it starts the scala REPL

% scala
scala> println("hello world")
hello world
scala> println("good-bye world")
good-bye world
^DC-R

The ^D above means control-D, a conventional way to signal end-of-input in a Unix context. C-R denotes 'Enter'. From now on scala> denotes we are in the REPL.
